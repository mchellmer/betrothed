Opening Ceremony

Colorado - 9-Feb-18

A small group of friends spanning the many stages of their respective lives will gather to witness Mark and Josephine's intent marry. Each will present a blessing to the bethrothed and any guidance to maintain a lifelong partnership. Mark and Josephine will then announce their partnership publicly, thus opening the officiation of marriage.

A Time of Reflection

Colorado - 9-Feb-18 through 16-Feb-18

With the ceremony officially open Mark and Josephine will spend a week together in Colorado reflecting on their decision to marry.

Closing Ceremony

New Jersey - 16-Feb-18

The ceremony will close with a mid-day gathering of Mark and Josephine's family. Mark and Josephine will present themselves as married and the respective families will affirm their recognition of this partnership by signing their names to the marriage pact. This will complete the legal ceremony and the couple will be recognised as wed. Food, drink, and a tour of the grounds will follow.
